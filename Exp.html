The Solution:

Building a Culture of Security:
Understanding that security is everyone's responsibility, I played an active role in educating and training employees on compliance responsibilities. This initiative helped build a culture of security awareness throughout the organization.
Collaboration at the Core:

My Role at Jefferies : Navigating the Frontlines of Cyber Defense
At Jefferies LLC, I joined the Security Operations Center (SOC) team as a Security Operation Analyst Intern a fast-paced environment where every second counted. From day one, I was thrust into the heart of the action, dealing with the constant ebb and flow of potential threats and security incidents.
The Challenge:
The digital landscape was bustling with activity, and the threat actors were relentless. Our SOC was the frontline defense against a myriad of cyber threats, ranging from malware and phishing attempts to more sophisticated attacks. My mission was to help safeguard the organization’s assets by swiftly detecting, analyzing, and responding to these incidents.
Diving Into Incident Response:
One of my primary responsibilities was conducting incident response triage. Over the course of my internship, I handled more than 300 security incidents, rapidly assessing the nature of each threat and escalating critical ones to ensure quick resolution. I became adept at distinguishing genuine threats from false positives, ensuring our team could focus on the incidents that mattered most.
Harnessing Powerful Tools:
To combat these threats, I leveraged advanced tools like VirusTotal and the CrowdStrike sandbox to monitor emails for malicious content. I analyzed suspicious attachments, domains, and SMTP logs to identify potential threats, catching them before they could cause any damage. This proactive approach helped us stay one step ahead of cybercriminals.
Network Forensics and Threat Hunting:
But the work did not stop at emails. I dug deeper, investigating incidents reported by Mandiant Intelligence Response**, Splunk, and **Security Onion**. Using network forensics techniques, I scoured through network traffic to uncover hidden threats, tracing their origins and pathways across our infrastructure. This hands-on experience gave me a solid foundation in understanding how different types of attacks unfold and how to counter them effectively.
Fighting Data Exfiltration and Ransomware:
With tools like Vectra and Symantec, I monitored for signs of data exfiltration watching closely for unusual outbound traffic that might indicate data being siphoned out of our network. When ransomware threats emerged, I employed Cryptospike to detect and mitigate these attacks swiftly, helping prevent potential encryption of critical data and minimizing the risk of operational downtime.
Automating for Efficiency:
To enhance our incident response capabilities, I implemented a playbook that integrated **Splunk** with ServiceNow. This integration automated service account lockouts whenever suspicious activity was detected, significantly streamlining our response process. The automation reduced manual workloads and improved our response time, ensuring threats were neutralized more quickly.
Collaborating for Secure Cloud Deployments:
Recognizing the importance of secure cloud operations, I collaborated closely with development teams to ensure that all cloud applications were deployed securely. I helped develop cloud security policies and best practices, making sure that our cloud environment remained resilient against evolving threats.
Managing Security Policies and Firewalls:
I also took on the crucial task of configuring and managing security policies for both on-premises and cloud firewalls. This included overseeing firewall patch management and lifecycle activities, ensuring that our defenses remained robust and up to date against the latest vulnerabilities.
The Outcome:
My time at Jefferies was marked by a series of challenges that demanded quick thinking, technical expertise, and collaboration. I left the role not only with a deeper understanding of security operations but also with a proven track record of enhancing security posture and resilience. It was an experience that solidified my passion for cybersecurity and my commitment to defending against the threats of tomorrow.
